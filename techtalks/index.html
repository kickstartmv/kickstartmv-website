Kickstart Techtalks

... are monthly events, organised by the not-for-profit group Kickstart, for sharing knowledge about important concepts in computer science and the latest developments in software development. Three speakers will deliver three talks - 2 x 15 min short talks + 1 x 30 min indepth talk - on topics of interest. Attendance is free to all but due to limited seat availability, interested participants are advised to RSVP ahead.

Learn important concepts, theories and ideas in computer science and software development.

Learn about the latest techniques, tools, libraries, frameworks and technologies.

Appear as a speaker and share your knowledge. Get respect and love from your peers.

Meet like minded people, exchange ideas, observe and learn from each other.
